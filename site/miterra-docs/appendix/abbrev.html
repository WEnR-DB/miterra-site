Abbreviations, Acronyms & Symbols

Terms

EU

Europen Union

EU27

The 27 member states of EU as of Feb. 1, 2020, i.e., Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, the Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, and Sweden.

NUTS

Nomenclature of Territorial Units for Statistics

NUTS1

NUTS Level 1. “Gewesten/Régions” in Belgium; “Länder” in Germany; “Continente”, “Região dos Açores” and “Região da Madeira” in Portugal.

NUTS2

NUTS Level 2. “Provincies/Provinces” in Belgium; “Comunidades y ciudades autónomas” in Spain; “Régions” in France; “Länder” in Austria.

NUTS3

NUTS Level 3. “Amtskommuner” in Denmark; “Départements” in France; “Län” in Sweden; “Megyék” in Hungary; “Kraje” in Czechia; “Oblasti” in Bulgaria.

PTF

Pedotransfer Function

SOC

Soil Organic Carbon

SOM

Soil Organic Matter

SON

Soil Organic Nitrogen
